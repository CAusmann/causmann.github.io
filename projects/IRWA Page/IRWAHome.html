Who is IRWA?
The International Right of Way Association (IRWA) is a global community of professionals involved in various aspects of right of way and infrastructure development. Founded in 1934, our members include experts in land acquisition, appraisal, real estate, law, engineering, and environmental studies. IRWA is committed to professional growth and excellence, providing education, certification, and networking opportunities to enhance the skills and knowledge of our members. We strive to promote fair practices and improve the quality of life through sustainable infrastructure development.
